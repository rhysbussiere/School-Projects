EXPERIENCES

LIFE AS A KID

My first experience with computers dates back to me as a kid, when my mom would bring home the work laptop. My brother and I would take turns playing the video game Diablo 2 while listening to music and surfing the web. This soon grew into a need to "hack" or "cheat" in which we would use code to give ourselves better stats and items within the game. I always had such a fascination with all of it, which continued to grow and grow as we soon got our own home computer. Then came the birth of social media (in my case - MySpace) which would revolutionize the way we communicate and link with people close by or even around the world! It grew from there, building my own computers and computers for friends until I finally realized that I needed to take my ever growing hobby and turn it into a career!

BEFORE DEVELOPING

Before deciding to turn a leaf and become a developer, my life was all about the automotive world. I attended school at Dunwoody College of Technology in Minneapolis, MN to be an Automotive Technician. I landed my first job at Inver Grove Honda in the parts department while I waited for a technician spot to open up. I soon realized that I was very good at parts, and decided to stay! I caught on very quickly and expanded my knowledge and experience wherever I possibly could, then took the job of "Wholesale Specialist", where I worked with hundreds of shops over the Midwest region of the United States. After six years I decided that I no longer wanted to live in the Midwest, and left my job at Inver Grove Honda in preparation of the move. I made the big move in the spring of 2015 and ended up in the Pacific Northwest - Oregon to be specific. There I landed a job at Wilsonville Toyota as a parts adviser. I have worked for Wilsonville Toyota since May of 2015 and currently work there part time while attending school full time at The Tech Academy studying software development and computer programming.

CURRENTLY

Currently I am attending school at The Tech Academy to learn the skills I will need to start my career in the technology field. As I work and complete projects at school, I will update my experience as I work my way towards a career I have been waiting for and unknowingly working for my whole life. Whether it be IT work, Networking or software development - I am excited to see what the future has in store for me!
